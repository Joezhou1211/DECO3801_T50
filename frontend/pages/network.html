We have analysised over Five million tweets, and use LDA model to analyse the Sentiment of tweets. It is clearly that there are 40% of the tweets are positive and 20% of them are neutral, 40% of them are negative. What's more, we found that the famous or influential people will influence other users. Below is the two network:
figure1: network
figure2: transmission chains
From figure1, it is clearly that the users are influenced by the influential people. When the influential people's tweet is negative, then the users who transpond this tweet will also show the negative emotion.

From figure2, the transmission chains show that the influence of Fake news. With the influence of Fake news, the publick will often become negative emotion, hence it is important to control the information spreading and reduce the panic of the public.
